The transfer itself is more complex than simple atmospheric leakage.
Pluto's gravity is weak, an escape velocity of just one point two one kilometers per second.

Gas molecules escaping near the sub-Charon point experience a gravitational gradient that focuses their trajectories, not randomly into space, but preferentially toward the companion world.
